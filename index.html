Brands

There are a lot of companys who are known for making saxophones ranging from entry level to professional level. The most reputable brands consist of:

  • Yamaha
  • Selmer Paris
  • Yanagisawa
  • Jupiter

Types

There are different types of saxophones. Each is known for providing a different sound and tone.The most common ones are:

  • Soprano
  • Alto
  • Tenor
  • Baritone

Pricing

Saxophones have a fairly broad range in pricing. It depends on the type of saxophone you chose as well as how the instrument is known to perform. Entry level saxophones can cost you anywhere from $300 to $1000. Intermediate saxophones can cost you anywhere from $1200 to $3000. Professional saxophones can cost you anywhere from $3000 to $10000(even for a used one).
